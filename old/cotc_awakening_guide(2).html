COTC Awakening Stone Priority Guide - Community Edition

How to Read This Guide

AS1-AS5: Awakening Stones 1 through 5
U10: Ultimate Level 10 (highest priority)
U1-U9: Ultimate Level 1-9 (lower priority)
A1: Awakening 1 (+30% base stats)
A2: Awakening 2 (unlock 4th skill slot)
A3: Awakening 3 (+1000 HP)
A4: Awakening 4 (exclusive accessory)
Shard: Exchange for awakening stone shards
Keep: Save for future use/decision
Priority Order: U10 uses stones first, then A1-A4
