Important Notes:

  • Priority Order: Generally, U10 > A4 > A3 > A1 for top tier units
  • Sharding Strategy: Units with both poor U10 (L1 or L9) AND poor A4 (Tier C/D) are ideal for sharding
  • A1 Priority: Almost always worth getting A1 for the 30% stat boost on units you use
  • Resource Management: 6 unwanted awakening stones = 6000 shards = 1 dupe of your choice
  • Future Planning: Keep stones for units that might get buffs or have niche uses
  • Meta Changes: This guide is based on current meta and may change with updates
