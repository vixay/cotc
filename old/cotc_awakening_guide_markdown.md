## Legend

- **AS1-AS5**: Awakening Stones 1 through 5 (maximum 5 per character)
- **U10**: Ultimate Level 10 (highest priority ultimate skill)
- **U1-U9**: Ultimate Level 1-9 (lower priority ultimate skills)
- **A1**: Awakening 1 (+30% base attributes)
- **A2**: Awakening 2 (unlock 4th skill slot)
- **A3**: Awakening 3 (+1000 HP)
- **A4**: Awakening 4 (exclusive accessory)
- **Shard**: Exchange for awakening stone shards (6 stones = 6000 shards = 1 chosen dupe)
- **Keep**: Save for future use/decision

## Important Notes

1. **Maximum 5 Awakening Stones**: Each character can use up to 5 awakening stones (AS1-AS5)

2. **Priority Logic**:
   - If U10 (L10): Use stones for Ultimate first, then A1-A4
   - If U9 or lower: Get A1-A4 first, shard the 5th stone
   - If both U10 and A4 are poor: Shard early

3. **Resource Management**: 
   - 6 unwanted awakening stones = 6000 shards = 1 chosen character dupe
   - Save shards for high-priority characters like Primrose EX, Auguste, A2

4. **A4 Accessory Value**:
   - S+ tier: Game-changing accessories (Auguste's Playwright's Quill, Primrose EX's accessory)
   - S tier: Excellent accessories worth prioritizing
   - A tier: Good accessories, worth getting eventually
   - B tier: Moderate accessories, lower priority
   - C/D tier: Weak accessories, often not worth the investment

5. **Future Considerations**:
   - Meta can change with updates
   - Some characters may receive buffs
   - New content might make certain characters more valuable
   - Keep stones for characters you're unsure about

6. **General Guidelines**:
   - A1 is almost always worth it for +30% base stats on used characters
   - A2 unlocks 4th skill slot - valuable for most characters
   - A3 provides +1000 HP - helpful for survivability
   - A4 accessories can be build-defining for some characters
   - U10 can dramatically change a character's usefulness
